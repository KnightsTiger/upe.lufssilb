Adventure Tour

14 Nights/ 15 Days Tour

Day 01


On Arrival at Bandaranayake International airport you will be welcomed by our representative
Drive towards Negombo
Afternoon relaxe on the beach
Accommodation at Negombo

Day 02


Breakfast at the hotel
Morning cycling tour in Negombo to explore fishing village - While you are cycling you can visit to one of fishing houses and taste cup of tea & thereafter cycling to inside the village and through coconut estate.
Drive towards to Anuradhapura
Evening a sightseeing tour around ancient city of Anuradhapura. The ancient capital of Anuradhapura reigned supreme for over a millennium as one of the greatest monastic cities of the ancient world famed for Buddhist culture and architectural achievements across Asia and Europe. Ruled by 113 successive kings and 4 queens, Anuradhapura was a majestic sprawl of magnificent palaces, ornate pleasure gardens, exquisite sculptures, and vast Buddhist temples. The three main ‘dagabas’ are amongst the biggest architectural creations of the ancient world smaller in size only to the pyramids at Giza.
Accommodation at Anuradhapura

Day 03


Early morning with packets of breakfast leave for Wilpattu National park jeep safari.
Wilpattu national park in Sri Lanka has the most leopard density when compared to other national parks, also the national park is among the top national parks in the world for its leopard population, Wilpattu national park is extremely high biodiversity, including 31 species of mammals, sloth bear (Melursus Ursinus inornatus), water buffalo (Bubalus bubalis), elephant (Elephas Maximus Maximus), Sambhur (Rusa unicolor unicolor), Spotted deer (Axis axis ceylonensis), Mongoose, and shrew are most known.
Drive towards to Sigiriya
Afternoon leisure at hotel
Accommodation at Sigiriya

Day 04


Breakfast at the hotel
Morning climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress
The ultimate icon of Sri Lanka’s historic past, the giant rock fortress of Sigiriya arises high above the flat landscape, surrounded by jungle on all sides. A testimony to the inventiveness of the ancient civilizations of the land, the once impenetrable stronghold of King Kassapa, ‘Sihagiri’ or Lion’s Rock as it was called then, is considered to be the 8th wonder of the world today
Afternoon hike to Pidurangala rock
Pidurangala Rock is adjacent to Sigiriya Rock and is the best viewing spot of Sigiriya Rock as it is only slightly lower. Pidurangala is a popular spot for sunrise as you can enjoy 360-degree views of not only the valleys but also Sigiriya Rock.
Accommodation at Sigiriya

Day 05


Breakfast at the hotel
Drive towards Trincomalee
Afternoon leisure at beach
Accommodation at Trincomalee

Day 06


Early morning with packets of breakfast leave for Dolphin & Whales watching
Trincomalee, the most popular east coast seaside hamlet in Sri Lanka famous for its powdery sandy beach and turquoise blue shallow waters. During the months from July to September calm seas provide the best opportunities to go to deep waters to see these marine giants, Blue Whales of Sri Lanka.Get on board with us to see Blue Whales in the East coast of Sri Lanka by sailing to their resident colonies
Afternoon leisure at beach
Accommodation at Trincomalee

This trail takes you though some streams, waterfalls, platues, cloudy forest.
